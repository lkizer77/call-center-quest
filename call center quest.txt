# 📞 Call Center Quest: Benefits Edition

**Call Center Quest** is a browser-based role-playing game designed to train and engage Customer Service Representatives (CSRs) in a fun, interactive way. Players take on the role of a CSR in training, navigating simulated benefit calls, quizzes, and mini-games to level up their skills and knowledge.

---

## 🎮 Game Features

- 🧑‍💼 **Role Play Simulation**: Handle realistic benefit-related calls and make decisions to progress.
- 🧠 **Knowledge Quizzes**: Test your understanding with "Name That Benefit" and "Keep Compliant".
- ☕ **Mini-Games**: Take a break with Coffee Break, Lunch Time Chef, and Self-Care activities.
- 🧥 **Level Progression**: Start as a Novice in a red jacket and rise through the ranks to Expert.
- ✨ **Animated UI**: Cartoon-style characters, XP gain effects, and interactive buttons.
